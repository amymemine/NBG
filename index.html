WWW (World Wide Web)

The World Wide Web (WWW), commonly known as the Web, is an information system
enabling documents and other web resources to be accessed over the Internet.
Documents and downloadable media are made available to the network through web servers
and can be accessed by programs such as web browsers.
Servers and resources on the World Wide Web are identified and located
through character strings called uniform resource locators (URLs).
